Safety and Risk

  • Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) - Hotel and Lodging: OSHA provides guidelines and resources for hotel staff to identify and address safety hazards in the workplace.

    Link: https://www.osha.gov/data/sic-manual


  • NSC Hospitality Program - The National Safety Council (NSC) provides resources and training to help hotel staff prevent workplace injuries and fatalities.

    Link: https://www.nsc.org/safety-training/workplace


  • Hotel Management Training - This online course offered by Alison provides an introduction to hotel management, including safety and risk management principles. The course is free and takes approximately 10-15 hours to complete.

    Link: https://alison.com/tag/hospitality-management

Customer Experience

  • Customer Service Institute of America - The Customer Service Institute of America (CSIA) offers training and certification programs for hotel staff on customer service excellence.

    Link: https://www.serviceinstitute.com/customer-service-training/


  • American Hotel and Lodging Educational Institute (AHLEI) - AHLEI offers a variety of training courses and certifications for hotel employees, including courses on customer service, front desk operations, and hospitality leadership.

    Link: https://www.ahlei.org/


  • inkedIn Learning - LinkedIn Learning provides a variety of courses on customer service and customer experience for hotel employees, including courses on customer service fundamentals, customer service communication, and customer experience design.

    Link: https://www.linkedin.com/learning/topics/customer-service-3

Food Handling and Safety

  • FDA Food Code - The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) provides guidelines and resources for food safety and handling in the hospitality industry. Their food code covers topics such as food preparation, storage, and service. https://www.fda.gov/food/fda-food-code

    Link: https://www.fda.gov/food/buy-store-serve-safe-food/safe-food-handling


  • National Restaurant Association - The National Restaurant Association provides training and resources for food handlers and managers in the hospitality industry.

    Link: https://restaurant.org/education-and-resources/learning-center/food-nutrition/
