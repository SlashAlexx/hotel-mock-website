Welcome to New Mexico's Sitzmark Chalet Inn!

Nestled beneath the tall pines of Ruidoso, Sitzmark Chalet hosts guests from near and far. Stay up late relaxing in our barrel sauna, and wake up to freshly brewed local Pinon coffee and warm muffins. But wait, there's more!

Ruidoso is surrounded by enchanting wilderness, beautiful mountains, exciting nightlife, and all the shopping your heart desires. You'll be talking about your stay with us for years to come!
